About

About me

Hello, I'm Philippe

I am a Software Developer with four years of experience in web and mobile projects using technologies such as Javascript, HTML, C#, ReactJS, React-Native and SQL/noSQL.

After receiving a bachelor degree in Information Systems, I started my career as a software developer in 2016 at PETROBRAS - The biggest company of Brazil, where I had my first professional experience with Javascript, HTML, C#, and SQL by working on a web application related to petroleum engineering.

In 2017, I accepted the challenge of creating a real-time chat application from scratch for a StartUp in Aracaju, Brazil. Since our team was focused on javascript-based technologies, we considered NodeJS and React-Native as our main stack, given that the app would be available for the Android and IOS platforms. That project was a great opportunity to improve my knowledge in Javascript (ES6) and data structures as well as learn new technologies and approaches such as React component life cycle and non-relational databases.

Since 2019 I have been working at Plexxis Software (Brampton, ON - Canada) as a full-stack mobile developer, providing solutions and improving a webview-based application for the construction industry...
