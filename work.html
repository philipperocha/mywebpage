AppZone

AppZone is a project under construction. It is a group chat application for IOS and Anroid which is being developed with React Native framework, NodeJs (Web API) and Google Firebase (Storage and Database).

Through the AppZone users can create groups or subscribe to them as well as find them based on their localization (Google Maps). Users can also filter groups by category and see how far they are. In addition, the AppZone allows sending requests to follow users and having private or group conversation.
